Hasty Maestro

Description

“HASTY MAESTRO” is a dynamic musical instrumentation and singing competition. Talented musicians and singers can compete across various genres under the time pressure. All branches (ex: Engineering, Arts, Medical Departments) are allowed to participate in the event.

Rules

  • INSTRUMENTATION (Solo & Band)
  • Participants has to bring their own instruments
  • Participants are allowed to showcase their skills for 5 minutes maximum
  • Karaoke should be submitted before the event starting

Quizzy Wood

Description

"Hello Fandom!!! Are you a weebs or Movie freaks. It’s time to choose Best Nerd. The contest where madness begins-The “QUIZZY WOOD” is all about Marvel, DC, Anime, Disney and all other Franchise. It’s a place to showoff your “Fictional Sense” with us, under constraints”. Round 1-Quiz (Selection round) Round 2-Scoring innings Round 3-Winning round.

Rules

  • Each candidate must register prior to the competition start time
  • Candidates are required to maintain respectful conduct throughout the competition
  • Mobile phones must be kept on silent mode during the competition
  • The quizmaster's decisions are final
